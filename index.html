About Company

eJuuZ is a community-first digital payments and marketplace platform built for township, peri-urban, and rural economies.

We make it easy for people to move from cash to digital spending by accepting trusted cash-send services, verifying funds, and enabling safe purchases from local merchants through pay-and-collect or delivery.

Our mission is to simplify everyday buying and selling, empower informal and micro-businesses with digital storefronts and tools (like invoicing, ticketing, and donations), and keep economic value circulating within local communities.

With a strong focus on trust, simplicity, and inclusion, eJuuZ helps communities trade, grow, and thrive—anywhere, anytime.
